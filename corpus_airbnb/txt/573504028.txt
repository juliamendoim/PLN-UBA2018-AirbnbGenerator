Erik was very kind and gave us all the information we needed. We used his taxi service to/from the airport for a very reasonable price. The flat itself is very spacious and comfortable, just as in the photos/description. It was quite cold when we were there, but with the heating in the room it was no problem at all. The bus to the Argentinian side of the falls stops at the corner and is very convenient, to go to the Brazilian side by public transportation you need to first get to the bus station and then take another bus with limited frequency, so a taxi may be a better option. All in all an excellent choice in Puerto Iguazú.
